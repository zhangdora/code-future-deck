Moat

Why We Win

Deterministic judging engine
Correctness is handled by an independent engine. AI never judges right from wrong — immune to hallucination. Built for China's regulatory regime — which makes it exportable by design: the same architecture keeps core assessment outside the EU AI Act's high-risk category.
Solutions never leak
Architecturally prevented from outputting full solutions, continuously verified. A coach’s most valuable asset is their problem set and methodology — we protect both. This is why institutions are willing to pay.
800 problems · 21 modules
A structured C++ knowledge graph drives precision practice. Not a chatbot wearing a problem bank — curriculum depth and coverage are the real moat.
Adaptive learning paths
Every student follows a personalized trajectory based on their level, weak points, and learning pace. The system adapts as they progress — no two students see the same path.
